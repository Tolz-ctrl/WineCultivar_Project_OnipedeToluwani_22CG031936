                        - 178 samples
                        - 3 cultivar classes (0, 1, 2)
                        - 13 original features (6 selected)

Selected Features:      1. Alcohol
                        2. Malic Acid
                        3. Ash
                        4. Alcalinity of Ash
                        5. Magnesium
                        6. Flavanoids

Algorithm:              Logistic Regression (Multiclass Classification)
                        - Solver: lbfgs
                        - Max iterations: 1000
                        - Random state: 42

Preprocessing:          StandardScaler (Feature Normalization)
                        - Fit on training data
                        - Transform both train and test data

Train/Test Split:       80% Training (142 samples)
                        20% Testing (36 samples)
                        - Stratified split
                        - Random state: 42

Model Performance:      Training Accuracy:   96.48%
                        Test Accuracy:       94.44%
                        Precision (macro):   95.83%
                        Recall (macro):      93.33%
                        F1-Score (macro):    94.07%

Model Persistence:      Method: joblib (Python pickle alternative)
